By leading you through a series of questions, PensionHelp America will put you on the path to finding an organization that can help you get answers to questions you may have about your retirement plan.

The Pension Rights Center

The Pension Rights Center is the nation's only consumer organization dedicated solely to protecting and promoting the retirement security of American workers, retirees, and their families. Visit the PRC website.

The Pension Counseling & Information Program

Funded by the U.S. Administration on Aging, six regional Pension Counseling projects help individuals understand their pension rights and claim the benefits they have earned, regardless of age or income. Services are provided free of charge. Learn more.
